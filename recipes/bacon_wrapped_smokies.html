Description

The Bacon Wrapped Smokies recipe from presents a delectable appetizer that combines savory and sweet flavors, making it a hit at any gathering. This dish involves wrapping beef cocktail wieners in bacon slices, securing them with toothpicks, and generously sprinkling brown sugar over the top. As they bake, the brown sugar caramelizes, creating a mouthwatering glaze that complements the smoky bacon and tender wieners.

To prepare, preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil for easy cleanup. Cut bacon slices into thirds, then wrap each piece around a cocktail wiener, securing it with a toothpick. Arrange the wrapped wieners on the prepared baking sheet and sprinkle them evenly with brown sugar. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40 minutes, or until the bacon is crisp and the sugar has melted into a bubbly glaze. These bite-sized treats can be served immediately or kept warm in a slow cooker, making them a convenient and crowd-pleasing option for parties and game-day events.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sliced Bacon, cut into thirds
  • 1 (14 ounce) package beef Cocktail wieners
  • Toothpicks
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line a large, rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  3. Wrap each cocktail wiener with a piece of bacon; secure with a toothpick. Place bacon-wrapped wieners on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Evenly sprinkle brown sugar over the tops of the bacon-wrapped wieners.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crisp and sugar is bubbly, about 40 minutes.
  6. Serve immediately, or place in a slow cooker set on Low to keep warm.
